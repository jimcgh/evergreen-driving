Mastering the Richmond ICBC Routes

Navigating the Richmond ICBC testing routes requires specific local knowledge. Examiners frequently test your observation skills around the busy Lansdowne area, where pedestrian traffic and complex intersections demand heightened awareness. We extensively cover tricky school zones and playground areas, such as those near Minoru Park, ensuring you automatically adjust your speed to 30 km/h. We also train you to confidently handle unmarked crosswalks along Granville Avenue and complex right-of-way scenarios near No. 3 Road.

The Secret to Perfect Parallel Parking

Mastering parallel parking is often the most anxiety-inducing part of the ICBC road test. Our instructors break it down into a foolproof, step-by-step mathematical approach. We teach you exactly where to align your vehicle, the precise reference points on your windows and mirrors, and when to counter-steer. Instead of guessing, you'll have a reliable formula that works on any vehicle size, ensuring you park within the required 30cm of the curb effortlessly.
